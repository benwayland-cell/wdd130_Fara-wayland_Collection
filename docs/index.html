This is Fara-Wayland Collection, a little website that just showcases several YouTube channels I find interesting. These are all human-curated channels that I think more people should know about. Some of them are small, some of them are big, but I think they all give value to this world, and I just want to share that with others.

Comedy

These channels are the funny ones. These are channels that have genuine humor to them. One making fun of the hype around the modern software industry, and one making fun of strange cultural differences in living in another country.

Animation

This is where there are really talented artists. They're just putting what they do up online for free. These include story-time animators, including some really small channels. There's also some telling a story and making a world in their art.

Educational

You might notice that this is the largest page on here, that's because I really like learning things. These have videos on AI and why they're so complex and potentially dangerous. There's also some talking about silly though experiments like “What if we drained the ocean?”, or “Can you draw every flag in Powerpoint?”.

Fun

These are the more fun channels. Some are wholesome, one is about meteor hammers, and another doing covers of video game music.
