Fun Channels

These are the more fun channels. Some are wholesome, one is about meteor hammers, and another doing covers of video game music.

Becorns | David M Bird

On this channel, David poses these little figures made of nuts, pine cones, and twigs that he calls Becorns. They're little woodland creatures that explore the world and have their own names and personalities. They're often posed beside woodland creatures such as birds or mice. He does a really great job telling a story with these pictures and telling of his experience taking them. I'd recommend this video where he tells of his experience making a figure out of flowers.

We Love You

We Love You is a wholesome channel that is mostly short-form hopecore videos. The videos are them talking to each other about various things such as the nature of life and how we can see hope in it. All of these videos have them exploring in a beautiful landscape in the outdoors. They also have recently started a podcast with the same general vibes that I quite enjoy. I'd recommend this video on how things of any size are not ordinary.

InstrumentManiac

InstrumentManiac does covers of various video game soundtracks. He does it all with real instruments which gives the song a unique vibe. I would recommend this cover of Choral Chambers from Hollow Knight: Silksong.

Instructor Bensei

Instructor Bensei makes short-form content about meteor hammer martial arts (metal ball on the end of a rope). He also runs a meteor hammer dojo. He also tries to do silly things with meteor hammers sometimes and often tries out weird meteor hammers people have sent him. He also likes to give bits of genuinely wholesome wisdom. I would recommend this video of him playing with a chain chomp meteor hammer that was sent to him.

Daily Deltarune News

If you like Deltarune you should watch this. This is a slightly jokey channel that gives daily updates on Deltarune news (which hardly ever happens) It's mostly hosted by one guy, but they often have guest hosts telling the sad fact that there is no news. There's also crazy people who comment on these videos every day. Sometimes they have special events when there is news or on a chapter release day.
