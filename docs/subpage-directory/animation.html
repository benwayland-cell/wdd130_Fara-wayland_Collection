Animation

This is where there are really talented artists. They are putting what they do online for free. This page includes story-time animators, small channels, and creators who build full worlds through animation.

Ice Cream Sandwich

Ice Cream Sandwich is a story-time animator who also posts funny question videos and animates audience responses. His videos are simple, expressive, and very easy to watch.

Vritziestar

Vritziestar is a newer story-time animator with a cute style and great drawing skills. She is a smaller creator with a lot of talent and very charming videos.

Coopymations

Coopymations is another recent story-time animator. His style is more relaxed and conversational, including videos about first-job experiences and everyday stories.

Luke Humphris

Luke Humphris creates world-focused animations with liminal spaces, strange environments, and darker themes that still carry a hopeful edge.

Its ok koy

Its ok koy animates over songs and quietly tells extra stories inside the visuals. The channel is expressive, emotional, and full of small details.

Jim EdLee

Jim EdLee is known for animated shorts and the You Cannot Run Forever series, mixing absurd comedy, cryptids, and road-trip style storytelling.
