## How did you manage your development team and comunicate the tasks for your junior devs?
I managed my development team by first assigning each developer a website that they would focus
on the development of and then gave small sub tasks that they should also do, like checking links
for validity and testing GitHub pages is displaying the website properly. Our main method of
communication was discord, but they could see their roles and tasks in GitHub issues too.

## How did you communicate with your client? Was there any issues?
The client and I mostly used discord, but we also communicated in person. There was no issues
and the communications were good.

## How did you handle publishing your clients site and sharing it with the client?
The client had created the base folder structure as outlined by the assignment, and from
there I created any additional files that we needed. Then, the development team and I would
push the changes to GitHub and I would send the link to the page via email.

## How was your experience with managing your site with GitHub?
GitHub works well most of the time, and I encountered no major issues with it. As long as
I consistently pulled changes and pushed updates I made, the site ran smoothly.
