
### How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?

I made a simple logo using some stock photos of books and powerpoint. The bulk of the images for the website were just taken from the channel banners that I highlighted and it's pretty easy to just grab them from YouTube.

### How did you communicate with your developer? Was there any issues with communication?

We had very little communication, but things have gone well enough I think.

### Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?

I think that the home page turned out well, but several other pages have inconsistent styling.

### What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?

Pay more attention to how the project is going and steer them in the right direction when they seem to stray from the vision.
